help Help Center

rocket_launch Getting Started

  • How to create your first blog: Click the "New Post" button in the dashboard, enter a title, write your content, and click "Publish".
  • How to edit & delete a blog: Use the edit (pencil) and delete (trash bin) buttons on any blog card on the dashboard.
  • How to use the dashboard: Access stats, browse drafts vs published posts, search, and view activity history using the floating dock and sidebar.

auto_awesome AI Writing Assistant

  • What is "Write with AI"? An integrated helper powered by Gemini to generate drafts or enhance your existing posts.
  • How to generate a blog with AI: In the editor page, type your topic in the "Write with AI" prompt box and click "Generate Draft".
  • How to pick AI-suggested titles: Simply click on any suggestion in the "SEO Title Suggestions" sidebar list to auto-write it into your blog title.
  • What does each tone mean?
    • Formal: Highly objective, precise, and professional.
    • Casual: Friendly, conversational, and easy to read.
    • Professional: Polished, structured, and business-ready language.

quiz FAQs

  • Can I edit AI-generated content? Yes! Once the AI draft is inserted, you can type, format, and edit it fully in the rich text area.
  • Is my blog data saved automatically? Yes! A debounced auto-save triggers on every edit and saves your progress to MongoDB.
  • How do I search for a blog? Type keywords in the header search bar to filter posts dynamically.
  • Can I recover a deleted blog? Blogs are moved to "Trash" first. You can view trash by clicking "Trash" in the sidebar.
  • What formats can I export in? PDF and JSON, accessible via Settings under the "Data & Privacy" tab.
